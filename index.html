Hel, The Goddess of Death

A super famous and rebel character in the Norse pantheon is the Goddess of Death, Hel. She played an essential role in judging the dead and had a significent influence in Norse eschatology. In the legend of Ragnarok, Hel led the terrifying army of dead in a ship that was made from the fingernails of corpses. This fierce goddess rules the whole underworld of Norse mythology as she brings the end of the world with her touch.
